Privacy Policy

Vaultpaw is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, track, or share any personal information, usage analytics, or device identifiers.

Subscription and purchase information is processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit; we do not receive or store your payment details.

Because Vaultpaw does not collect data, there is no data to access, export, or delete on our servers — everything lives in the app's local storage on your device and is removed if you delete the app.
